1.

Computer Lab

Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, established in 1987, offers B.Tech., M. Tech. and Ph.D. degrees. The department has well qualified and motivated faculty members and support staff. The laboratories are adequately equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. The department is actively involved in R&D as well as consultancy projects and has collaborations with several industries, academic institutes and R&D organizations in the country. The first B. Tech batch graduated in 1991 and the first M.Tech. batch graduated in 2009. There are more than 20 full-time research scholars registered in the department. The department has good laboratory facilities with adequate number of desktops. Different types of servers like those by IBM, Apple, HP, Dell, etc. are installed in the laboratories. The laboratories are open round the clock and are operated and managed by the students themselves. The students also get access to laser and ink-jet printers. For research work, the department regularly procures specialized hardware and software packages as required for the specific research project. The institute has a very good networking facility.

Head of the Department: Dr.P.S.Deshpande

2.

Chemistry Lab

Chemical Department

Chemical engineers combine mathematics, chemistry, physics, biology, and economics to tackle challenging fundamental and large-scale industrial problems by developing theories, advanced simulations, and experiments. The club provides a platform for young minds to experiment and innovate, and come up with something extra ordinary solutions for the present and futuristic challenging scientific and societal problems. Indian Institute Of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) is a confluence of streams of professionals from academia, research institute and industry. It provides them the appropriate forum for joint endeavors, hand-in-hand, to work for human being through application of chemical engineering and allied sciences. This department has a good result.It has a very good amount of placement percentage. This department has very good faculties. It has very good labs. Labs of this departmentare fully furnished. There are all the equipments needed in the lab.Chemical Lab is a very big hall having fascilities for research and development.

Head of the Department: Dr.Sachin A. Mandavgane

3.

Physics Lab

Department of Physics

Department of Physics is involved in teaching for UG and PG students of Engineering. M.Sc. (Physics) course started in 2012 lays thrust on fundamental physics and also on applied Physics courses. In M.Sc. course, the project work has a significant weightage which is expected to enhance the quality of project work and give motivation towards research. The department has highly qualified and experienced faculty who guide UG, PG and Ph.D. students. The department has number of ongoing as well as completed R&D projects. The areas of research include Solid State Ionics, Nanomaterials, Functional Glasses and Glass Ceramics, Electroceramics, Magnetic materials, Polymers, Computational Physics, Photovoltaics, sensors, supercapacitors & optoelectronic devices. The department has sophisticated instrumentation facilities for synthesis, characterization & applications.

Head of the Department: Dr.V.K.Deshpande
